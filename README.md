### 🏁 Race Calendar
Race Calendar is a stripped-down, high-speed motorsport tracker built for people who actually care about the racing—not the noise around it. Riders, builders, and fans get a clear line of sight into the season without wading through corporate sludge.
It pulls together race events from MotoGP, Formula 1, WorldSBK, TT, and BSB, laying them out in a clean, ruthless format—dates, locations, and series filtering—everything you need, nothing you don’t.

Built inside the MM Racing garage, this thing runs on a few simple principles:
Speed – get in, get the info, get out
Clarity – no clutter, no nonsense
Focus – races, dates, and series, nothing extra
Extensibility – built to grow as the madness expands

### 🗂️ Supported Series
MotoGP – Premier class motorcycle racing
Formula 1 – Global open-wheel championship
WorldSBK – Production-based superbike racing
Isle of Man TT – Road racing at its most extreme
British Superbike (BSB) – One of the most competitive national series

### ⚙️ Features
Multi-series race calendar (MotoGP, F1, WSBK, TT, BSB)
Series filtering to quickly switch between championships
Next race highlight with countdown
Race status indicators (upcoming / completed)
Circuit names and locations for each event
Clean, fast interface built for real-world use
Lightweight design – no bloat, no loading nonsense

This wasn’t born in a boardroom. It started as a tool to keep track of race weekends between wrenching sessions and late nights in the garage. Now it’s turning into something bigger—a proper motorsport companion for people who live this stuff, not just watch it.
